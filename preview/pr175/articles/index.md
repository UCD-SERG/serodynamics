- [Fitting the model with Stan, and how it compares to
  JAGS](https:/ucd-serg.github.io/serodynamics/preview/pr175/articles/jags-to-stan.md):

  serodynamics can fit the within-host antibody model with two MCMC
  engines: JAGS (Gibbs sampling) and Stan (Hamiltonian Monte Carlo).
  This article explains how the Stan model is built from the JAGS model,
  how to use it, and shows that the two engines give the same answer
  where the data are informative.
